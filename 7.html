Travis Scott: Jacques Webster Jr. (born April 30, 1992), known professionally as Travis Scott, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. He is best known for his unique blend of hip-hop, trap, and psychedelic music, which has garnered widespread critical acclaim and commercial success. Scott first gained recognition with his 2013 mixtape "Owl Pharaoh," and went on to release several more successful mixtapes, including "Days Before Rodeo" and "Rodeo." His full-length studio albums, including "Rodeo," "Birds in the Trap Sing McKnight," and "Astroworld," have been commercial and critical successes, with the latter becoming his first number-one album on the Billboard 200 chart. In addition to his music, Scott is known for his energetic live performances and innovative stage designs, which often feature elaborate lighting and special effects. He has received numerous awards and nominations, including BET Hip Hop Awards, Billboard Music Awards, and Grammy Awards nominations.
